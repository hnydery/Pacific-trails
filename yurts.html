The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanet structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, a roof dome that can be opened, and a privaye deck.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a gas fireplace, king-size bed with down quilt, comfy couch, dining table and chairs, mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker. Your luxury camping experience icludes a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring>
Most guests pack comftorable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
